Our Story

About LIKLUK

Over 17 years of expertise in automotive parts manufacturing and international trade — built on quality, trust, and long-term partnerships.

Who We Are

A Trusted Name in Global Auto Parts Supply

Founded in 2009 in Chongqing — China's automotive heartland — LIKLUK Automotive Technology Co., Ltd. has grown from a focused drivetrain specialist into a comprehensive auto parts manufacturer and exporter serving buyers across four continents.

We supply five core product systems: drivetrain, engine, gearbox, chassis, and model-specific parts for major Chinese vehicle brands. With over 10,000 SKUs in our catalogue, we serve importers, distributors, and trading companies who need a reliable single-source partner for their sourcing needs.

Our export markets include Europe, North Africa, the Middle East, and South America — regions where Chinese-brand vehicles have achieved significant market penetration and where demand for quality aftermarket parts continues to grow rapidly.

Our Journey

Company Milestones

2009
Company Founded

Established in Chongqing with a focus on drivetrain components — clutch kits, flywheels, and hydraulic cylinders.

2012
First International Exports

Began exporting to North Africa and Middle East markets as Chinese-brand vehicle adoption accelerated globally.

2015
Product Line Expansion

Expanded into engine system components and gearbox parts to meet growing demand for complete vehicle coverage.

2018
European Market Entry

Established partnerships with European distributors as Chinese vehicles gained significant European market share.

2021
Chassis & South America Expansion

Launched comprehensive chassis system product line. Developed South American distributor network.

2025
10,000+ SKU Catalogue

Completed comprehensive model-specific parts coverage for Changan, Geely, Chery, Dongfeng, and JAC vehicles.
